title = "Raspberry Ice Cream"

* 1 pint heavy cream 
* 1 quart raspberries 
* 1 cup granulated sugar

In a blender, combine cream, raspberries, and sugar. Blend until mixture roughly doubles in size. Pour into a quart container and freeze.
