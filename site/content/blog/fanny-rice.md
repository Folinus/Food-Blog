* 1 1/2 cups medium or long-grain rice 
* 1 medium onion, chopped 
* 1 shrimp-flavored bouillon cube 
* 2 1/4 cup water

Heat olive oil in a wide pan. Saut√© onion until soft. Add bouillon, cooking until gently browned. Add rice and stir, coating grains with olive oil. Add water and stir, then cover and cook over low-medium heat until rice is tender.
