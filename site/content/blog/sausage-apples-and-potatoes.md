* 1 Granny Smith apple, sliced 
* 2 tbsp olive oil 
* 1 medium onion, sliced 
* 4 sweet Italian sausages, split 
* 4 medium potatoes, cubed 
* 3 tbsp butter 
* 1/2 cup dry white wine

In a heavy fry pan with a cover, cook the sausages. Remove from heat and set aside. Add 1 tbsp of butter and melt, then saut√© onion on low until soft. Add potato and apples. Cover and steam for 30 minutes or until potato is cooked. Add sausages and remaining butter, melting to form a gravy for serving.
